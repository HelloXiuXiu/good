jez!

with DPG Media

JEZ! stands for Jong en Zot (young & crazy).
It is Belgian’s biggest fund raiser for youth causes. The concept celebrates the crazy energy of young people as a force for the future.
Deliverables: name, concept, key executions and socials, with DPG’s main brands -VTM, QMusic. HLN- & the support of Belfius. More than 3 million euros was collected for over 200 NGO’s and organisations in the first year.
