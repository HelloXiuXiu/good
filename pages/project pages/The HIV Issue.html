the hiv-issue

for Vangardist with Saatchi&Saatchi

The HIV-issue is magazine printed with the blood of HIV-positive people. It tells the story of HIV and its stigma today. It took 7 years to produce this idea, because of legal and technical obstacles. The magazine has been discussed in every media outlet and is on display in some of the most famous contemporary art and design musea.

Awarded with Cannes Gold, Eurobest Grand Prix, Best of Innovation
